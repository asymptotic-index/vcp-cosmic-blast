# References
Cosmic Blast — Venus Cooling Project

---

## Purpose

This document lists **scientific and educational references** relevant to the
Cosmic Blast analysis. Sources focus on planetary atmospheres, impact physics,
gravitational escape, and large-scale energy accounting.

The intent is to ground extreme scenarios in well-established physics.
